PaleroSoft is your all-in-one project management solution designed for modern teams. Whether you're managing a small startup or a large enterprise, PaleroSoft helps you stay organized, collaborate effectively, and deliver results.

## Core Features

### 📊 Project & Task Management
- Create and organize unlimited projects
- Break projects into manageable tasks
- Set priorities, deadlines, and assignments
- Track progress with real-time updates
- Visualize timelines with the calendar view

### 💬 Team Collaboration
- Real-time chat with team members
- Schedule and manage meetings
- Leave comments and feedback
- Get instant notifications for updates
- Share files and documents

### 💰 Financial Management
- Create professional invoices
- Track invoice status (Draft, Sent, Paid, Overdue)
- Manage client information
- View financial reports

### 📈 Analytics & Reporting
- Track team productivity
- Monitor project progress
- Generate custom reports
- Analyze team performance metrics

- Functions offline with auto-sync
- Install like a native app on your home screen
- Automatic updates without app store delays
- No installation required - just open in browser
